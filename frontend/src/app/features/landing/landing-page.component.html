Our Story

Built for Nairobi,
Powered by Community

NduthiRide started from a simple idea: moving through the city should feel predictable, fair, and safe. We bring together vetted riders, transparent pricing, and tools for passengers and senders — so whether you are commuting or shipping a parcel, you always know what to expect.

Our team works closely with operators on the ground to improve routes, response times, and support — because great urban mobility is a partnership, not a one-way service.
